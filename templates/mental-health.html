What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does therapy take? expand_more

Treatment duration varies significantly based on individual goals. Short-term solution-focused therapy may last 8-12 weeks, while deep developmental work can continue for several months or longer.

Is it confidential? expand_more

Yes. Patient confidentiality is protected by HIPAA regulations. Exceptions only occur if there is an immediate risk of harm to yourself or others, or under specific legal mandates.

Do I need a psychiatrist? expand_more

A psychiatrist is necessary if medication management is required. Many patients find success with a combination of a therapist (talk therapy) and a psychiatrist (medical management), or therapy alone.
