A clinical approach to health using food and nutrients to manage diseases and improve physiological function. Through precision analysis and personalized metabolic adjustments, we target the root causes of dysfunction.

What is Nutrition Therapy?

At its core, Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT) is an evidence-based medical approach to treating certain chronic conditions through the use of an individually tailored nutrition plan. This process involves a comprehensive assessment of the patient’s nutritional status followed by treatment which includes counseling and use of specialized diets.

Risks & Contraindications

  • close Acute Trauma: Not suitable for acute surgical trauma requiring immediate parenteral nutrition.
  • close Severe Disorders: Not recommended for individuals with active, severe eating disorders without multidisciplinary support.
  • close Interactions: Specific drug-nutrient interactions must be monitored by a pharmacist.
