What is Naturopathy?

A distinct primary health care profession that emphasizes prevention, treatment, and optimal health through the use of therapeutic methods and substances that encourage individuals' inherent self-healing process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Naturopathy the same as Homeopathy? expand_more
No. While both use natural substances, Naturopathy is a broad system of primary care that includes nutrition, physical medicine, and botanical medicine. Homeopathy is a specific therapeutic system based on the "like cures like" principle using highly diluted substances.
Are Naturopathic Doctors licensed? expand_more
Licensing varies by jurisdiction. In many regions, Naturopathic Doctors (NDs) must graduate from accredited 4-year medical programs and pass professional board exams to be licensed as primary care providers.
