Siddha: The Path to Perfection

Ancient wisdom for timeless wellness. Siddha medicine is a traditional South Indian healing system that balances physical, mental, and spiritual health through natural alchemy.

Universal Foundations

Siddha medicine originated in the pre-Vedic period of South India. Its philosophy is built upon the **96 Tattvas**, which are categorized principles governing the human soul, body, and senses. This clinical system aims to achieve **Ashta Siddhis** (Eight Divine Perfections) by maintaining equilibrium within the body's constitution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Siddha different from Ayurveda? expand_more
While both share elements and humors, Siddha has a unique focus on mineral-metallic formulations (Iatrochemistry) and a distinct 96-tattva philosophy rooted in Tamil culture.
Are the metallic medicines safe? expand_more
Siddha medicine utilizes rigorous purification processes called 'Suddhi'. When prepared by qualified practitioners according to traditional canons, these minerals are bio-transformed into non-toxic, therapeutic agents.
What is 'Pathyam'? expand_more
Pathyam refers to the strictly prescribed diet and lifestyle regimen required to allow the medicine to work effectively. It is considered 50% of the treatment in Siddha.
