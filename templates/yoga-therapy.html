Yoga Therapy: Harmony for Health

"Ancient Wisdom Meets Clinical Wellness"

Yoga Therapy is a professional healthcare discipline that applies the principles and practices of yoga to health challenges. Unlike a general yoga class, therapy is highly personalized, focusing on clinical assessment and the management of chronic physiological and psychological conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I practice? expand_more
Clinical outcomes generally improve with a consistent practice of 3-4 times per week, though even 15 minutes of daily focused breathwork can provide significant autonomic benefits.
Do I need to be flexible to start? expand_more
No. Flexibility is a byproduct, not a prerequisite. Yoga therapy is designed to meet your body where it is currently, utilizing props and modifications to ensure safety and comfort.
Is it covered by insurance? expand_more
Coverage varies. Many holistic health plans and HSA/FSA accounts allow for Yoga Therapy when prescribed by a physician as part of a chronic pain management or cardiac rehab program.
