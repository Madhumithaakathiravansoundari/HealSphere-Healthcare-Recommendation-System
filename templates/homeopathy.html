Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see results? expand_more
Result timing varies significantly. Acute conditions may respond within hours, whereas chronic conditions usually follow a 1-month-per-1-year-of-illness recovery guideline. Consistency with the remedy is essential.
Are there any side effects? expand_more
Homeopathic remedies are highly diluted and typically do not cause toxic side effects. However, some patients experience a temporary "healing crisis" where symptoms slightly intensify before improving—this is considered a sign of the body engaging its healing mechanism.
